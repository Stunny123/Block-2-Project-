About Us

Methods

Sunlight is reflected with mirrors positioned at a certain angle so the sunlight can touch the pan and heat it by causing the particles to vibrate. Mirrors reflect the light because they’re electrically conductive (light is an electromagnetic field). The law of reflection states light is reflected at the same angle as it touches the surface.
